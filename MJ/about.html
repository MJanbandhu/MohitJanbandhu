About Me

I am Mohit Janbandhu, a Certified Data Scientist with proven expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. With hands-on experience in real-world projects and an in-depth understanding of data processing pipelines, I combine problem-solving skills with industry tools to deliver impactful solutions.

My certifications include recognition from IABAC and NASSCOM. I’m passionate about building intelligent systems, experimenting with LLMs and BERT, and contributing to open-source projects. I also actively participate in platforms like Kaggle to keep up with evolving trends in data science.

Beyond modeling, I focus on interpretability, performance optimization, and intuitive visualization, ensuring that insights are both actionable and understandable.

My goal is to leverage data to drive decision-making, improve automation, and contribute to innovative AI solutions across industries.
